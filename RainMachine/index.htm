RainMachine irrigation for 8 Ferebee Way

Scene of the crime
commercial sod with plastic netting over impervious silty clay;
about an inch of roots and sand to hold water..

- sand in roots over impervious subsoil quickly saturates
- neighborhood sprinklers typically runoff into streets

RainMachine waters zones based on soil type and weather,
instead of conventional Orbit or Rain Bird fixed times.
Pausing each zone, letting water soak in while cycling thru others,
mostly eliminates runoff.

Rain Bird sprinkler maintenance

Three common failure modes

leakage - riser seal failure
sand - maintain a small moat around heads to reduce wear
can replace riser without fully digging up
mower damage
bury deeper
nozzle failure
they are replaceable

Two flavors

5004 with check valve
3/4" pipe thread, nozzle inserts in head
head is integrated with riser
1804
1/2" pipe thread, head with nozzle screws into riser
